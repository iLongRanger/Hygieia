Hygieia System Flow

End-to-end business management for commercial and residential cleaning operations

Lead-to-Revenue Pipeline
1 — CRM
Lead Intake
  • Create or import lead
  • Assign to sales rep
  • Track source & type
  • Commercial or Residential
→
2 — CRM
Walk-Through
  • Schedule appointment
  • On-site assessment
  • Capture facility details
  • Areas, fixtures, conditions
→
3 — CRM
Account Setup
  • Convert lead to account
  • Create contacts
  • Set up facility profile
  • Assign pricing plan
→
4 — SALES
Proposal / Quote
  • Generate pricing
  • Add services & items
  • Send for approval
  • Customer reviews online
→
5 — SALES
Contract
  • Auto-create from proposal
  • Digital signature
  • Set schedule & terms
  • Auto-renewal tracking
→
6 — OPS
Job Execution
  • Auto-schedule from contract
  • Assign to team/worker
  • Complete task checklists
  • Log time & notes
→
7 — FINANCE
Invoice & Pay
  • Generate invoices
  • Send via public link
  • Record payments
  • Track aging & revenue
Commercial vs. Residential Tracks
Commercial Office, Retail, Warehouse
1 Lead & Appointment
↓
2 Account & Facility Setup
↓
3 Proposal (dynamic pricing)
↓
4 Customer Reviews & Signs
↓
5 Contract (amendments supported)
↓
6 Jobs, Inspections, Invoices
Residential Houses, Condos, Apartments
1 Account & Property
↓
2 Home Profile (beds, baths, sqft)
↓
3 Residential Quote (plan-based pricing)
↓
4 Customer Accepts Online
↓
5 Contract (auto-converted)
↓
6 Jobs, Inspections, Invoices
Both tracks converge into shared operations

User Roles & Access
Owner
  • Full system access
  • User management
  • System settings
  • Pricing configuration
  • All CRM & Sales
  • All Finance & Reports
Admin
  • User & team management
  • Settings & configuration
  • CRM read/write/admin
  • Sales read/write/admin
  • Operations management
  • Finance approve
Manager
  • CRM read/write
  • Sales management
  • Scheduling & appointments
  • Time tracking approval
  • Expense approval
  • No user/settings admin
Cleaner
  • Dashboard (view)
  • Assigned jobs & tasks
  • Time tracking (own)
  • Payroll (view own)
  • No CRM or sales access
  • No pricing or settings
Subcontractor
  • Same as Cleaner, plus:
  • Expense tracking (own)
  • Tier-based pay rates
  • No time tracking approval
  • No CRM or sales access
  • No pricing or settings
